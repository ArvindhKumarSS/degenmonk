Meet the Cast

01 / 07

The Monk

The Protagonist · Zen Trader

The stoic center of the DegenMonk universe. Serene, bald, draped in saffron. Sits cross-legged with a laptop, meditating on charts as if they were scripture. Approaches crypto with Buddhist detachment — holds through crashes without blinking, exits with the same stillness he entered. Never reveals his entries.

"Short the noise. Long the silence."
02 / 07

Degen

The Foil · Retail Psyche Incarnate

Disheveled hoodie plastered in coin logos. Wide manic eyes, perpetual sweat, phone permanently attached to hand showing a green chart. Buys the top. Sells the bottom. Lives in the group chat asking "wen moon?" The collective madness of retail trading distilled into one frantic human being.

"Wen moon? It's different this time."
03 / 07

The Whale

Silent Power · Market Mover

A massive shadowy figure in a pinstripe suit. Face never shown. Only enormous hands hover over a Bloomberg terminal. Accumulates in silence while others sell in panic. When the Whale types, markets move. The Monk respects but never fully understands The Whale. The fearful give their keys to the patient.

​
04 / 07

The Fed Chair

Bumbling Authority · Accidental Chaos Agent

Bald, gray suit, permanently bewildered. Carries a comically oversized money printer labeled BRRR. Every word he utters accidentally nukes or pumps the market. Thinks crypto is a passing fad. Genuinely believes he controls inflation. The Monk watches his press conferences with quiet amusement.

"Data dependent. Transitory. We have the tools."
05 / 07

The Altcoin Pilgrim

Eternal Optimist · True Believer

Colorful patchwork vest covered in coin logos, always clutching a whitepaper like holy scripture. Big permanent grin. Turns bullish at exact tops. Never holds more than a week. Blames the Monk for "not seeing the vision." Has attended 40 crypto conferences and still hasn't taken profit once.

"This one's different. Read the tokenomics."
06 / 07

Fibonacci

The TA Wizard · Pattern Seeker

Wizard hat covered in golden spirals. Six monitors, all buried in lines, retracement levels, and Elliott Wave counts. Chalk-dusted hands draw fibonacci spirals on everything — charts, napkins, his own arm. Right for the wrong reasons. Calls every bottom approximately three days after the bounce.

"The 0.618 retracement was obvious."
07 / 07

The Normie

Retail FOMO · Every Cycle's Victim

Plain clothes. Confused expression. Holding a newspaper screaming BTC ALL-TIME HIGH. Heard about crypto from their barber. Buys the top with conviction. Sells the bottom in despair. Swears off crypto forever. Returns next bull cycle. The market's most reliable indicator — when The Normie arrives, the top is in.

"My uncle says this is the next internet."
